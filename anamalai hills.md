anaimalais or anamala, also known as [[elephant]] mountains, are a range of mountains in southern western ghats and span border of western [[tamil nadu]] (coimbatore district and tiruppur district) and central [[kerala]](palakkad district, [[thrissur]] district, ernakulam district, and idukki district) in southern india. name anamala is derived from tamil word aanai, malayalam word aana, meaning elephant, or from tribal languages. mala or malai means 'hill', and thus 'elephant hill'.[3]
