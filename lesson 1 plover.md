### Solution: Consonant Clusters In Single-Stroke Words

-   '**course**': **KORS**
-   '**cover**': **KOFR**  
-   '**hover**': **HOFR** 
-   '**rabble**': **RABL** 
-   '**refer**': **REFR** 
-   '**rebel**': **REBL** 
-   '**robbed**': **ROBD** 
-   '**rubbed**': **RUBD**
-   '**rubble**': **RUBL**
-   '**straps**': **STRAPS** 
-   '**strapped**': **STRAPD**
-   '**trouble**': **TRUBL** 
-   '**troubles**': **TRUBLS** 
-   '**waft**': **WAFT** 
-   '**webbed**': **WEBD**
